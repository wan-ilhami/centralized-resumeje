## The Problem

Most resumes never reach a human — they're filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) because of small formatting issues, weak phrasing, or missing keywords. **ResumeJe fixes that.**

Upload your resume, and AI rewrites it to be clearer, stronger, and ATS-friendly — keeping your voice. Match it against any job description and get a real score with specific feedback. Export to a clean, recruiter-ready PDF in seconds.

---

## Features

- **📄 AI Resume Enhancement** — Upload PDF/DOCX/TXT; AI restructures and sharpens.
- **📊 ATS Score Checker** — Real compatibility score with actionable feedback against any job description.
- **✏️ Section Rewriter** — Strengthen any single section with one click.
- **💌 Cover Letter Generator** — Tailored cover letter in seconds.
- **📥 PDF Export** — Clean, recruiter-ready output.
- **🔒 Privacy First** — No account required; resume data stays in your browser.
